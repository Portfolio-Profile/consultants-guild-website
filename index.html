Service

Management & Business Consultancy.

Our aim is to provide objective advice and assistance relating to the strategy, structure, management and operations of an organisation in pursuit of its long-term purposes and objectives. Our experience tells that many endeavors or projects fail or does not give the desired results due to hastily done planning, inappropriate departmental/ process structures and lack of proper management level supervision. We feel we can, based on our professional experience, advise managements on key features of their business strategies and plans focusing on long-range planning, reorganisation of a company’s structure, if considered necessary, and a general business appraisal of the company

Book Keeping and Accounting

Every business requires data on how it performed in a given period. This information helps businesses identify the problem areas and hence make necessary improvements. Book keeping and Accounting is the activity which provides this information and data. It also helps the taxation side as the same data is used to file returns etc. We offer businesses the opportunity to outsource, if they already have a department or function defined in their organisation, or rely on us and give us the responsibility of handling this important function for them. Businesses can benefit from computerized accounting setups. We have the knowledge as well as the ability to provide tailored softwares, if they so desire, or use off-the-shelf solutions. All you have to do is discuss with us, and we will suggest and provide the best suitable option for you. If you desire, we can handle the function for you so that you can focus on your business instead of worrying about ledgers, payrolls and bank reconciliations.

Policies, Procedures and Process Development Services

It’s a known fact that standardizing procedures and practices help avoiding wastage of precious time and money. Well defined procedures can help managements avoid duplications in work, mistakes which occur due to ignorance of how to handle a situation. It pays to have standard operating procedures defined and placed to make compliance of laws and instructions possible.Having manuals, like accounting manual, security manual etc gives the staff clear instructions on day to day operating activities. It reduces time wastages, we have often observed, in obtaining instructions or even deciding whom to approach to get instructions. Having well set procedures give independence to management to focus on more important aspects of businesses than worrying about day to day choirs
